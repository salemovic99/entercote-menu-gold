The Secret of Geneva

CHEZ BOUBIER 1930

The Café de Paris sauce was created in Geneva in 1930 by Chef Boubier, and became a secret known to only a few. In 1942 the recipe passed to his daughter and her husband, beginning a legacy carried down through the generations, preserved without alteration.

Today, Mr. François Vuilloud continues to prepare the original Café de Paris sauce in Geneva, using carefully selected ingredients from his own farm, before it begins its journey to your table.

What we serve is not merely a recipe, but a heritage spanning more than ninety-five years — kept across generations to reach you today exactly as it began in Geneva.
